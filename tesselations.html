Tesselations

Should all go to plan, a play of relations should come out eventually...

In this play, all actors are begotten by the symbolic parents of relation. The mother of form is played by a boundary delineating a shape, accompanied by the father, a function enlivening the structure outlined by his spouse. Together they slice with life and graciously gift it to wherever difference can find foot. In other words, this play is a family drama begotten by the chymical marriage of boundaries and functions and articulated by their offspring into an alphabet of existence...
